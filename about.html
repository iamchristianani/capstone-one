NUBIACORE ART &
MUSIC FESTIVAL 2022

The Music festival is ecpected to bring together a large crowd filled with lovers of Afrobeats music, and arts in general. We expect to have our largest gathering of artiste and actors for maximum fun and enjoyment.

The amazing group of artiste has been expanded to include both the music pioneers and the best of this generation.

This festival is a first of it's kind in the country, where all aspects of entertainment would be covered, be it Music, Drama, Art, Dance and many more.
